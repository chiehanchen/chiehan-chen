The Introduction

I'm a junior at the University of Texas at Austin, majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a focus on Computer Architecture and Embedded Systems. I am currently working as a Undergraduate Research Assisstant at Dr. Constantine Caramanis's Applied Machine Learning Lab. Additionally, I have interned as a software engineering intern and software development intern at Code2College in summer 2020 and summer 2021 respectively.

Some projects I have worked on are Epic Escape, AceIt, and MusicLover

Epic Escape: An embedded systems project built using C/C++, Arm Assembly, TM4C Microcontroller, Color TFT LCD Display - ST3775R. It is a virtual escape room running on a TM4C microcontroller with vivid visuals displayed on the LCD screen to assist players navigate through the room using 4 controlled buttons. Appropriate sound effects are embedded in the program for better user experience and implemented through a DAC.

AceIt: A website built using Python, JavaScript, HTML/SCC to connect high school students to additional free educational resources and tutors based on their geolocation. Won 2nd place at the Web Design Demo Day Competition with a scholarship from Google

MusicLover: A mobile social media app developed using Reactjs and Firebase for music lovers to share their favorite music and create discussion posts (runs on both iOS and)
